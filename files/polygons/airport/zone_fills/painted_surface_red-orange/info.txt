Title: Painted Surface, Red-Orange
=====================
Author: Cormac Shaw
URL: http://www.xpih.net
Revision: $Revision$
Date: $Date$
=====================
Description: A repeating solid-coloured surface marking depicting a red-orange asphalt overlay. Typically used to indicate traffic lanes or other areas that are limited to a particular usage, e..g., a bus lane.

This polygon is drawn at the 'Runways -1' level so will appear below all  default markings in X-Plane generated by apt.dat files.
