Description: A large-sized airport catering truck with platform raised to 4.0m in Gate Gourmet markings.
The vehicle is based on a Mercedes 3-axle chassis with a full size cab. The capacity and configuration is optimised for use with mid to large size airliners. Loader platform is raised to 4.0m, suitable for aircraft types B757, A310.
