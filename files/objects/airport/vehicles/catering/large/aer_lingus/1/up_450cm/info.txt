Description: A large-sized airport catering truck with platform raised to 4.5m in Aer Lingus markings.

The vehicle is based on a Mercedes 3-axle chassis with a full size cab. The capacity and configuration is optimised for use with mid to large size airliners. Loader platform is raised to 4.5m, suitable for aircraft types B767, A330.
