Description: A mid-sized airport catering truck with platform raised to 4.0m in red markings.
The vehicle has a modified half-size drivers cab on a Mercedes 2-axle chassis allowing access to low-silled aircraft. Loader platform is raised to 4.0m, suitable for aircraft types B757, A310.
