Description: A mid-sized airport catering truck with platform raised to 3.1m in Servair markings.
The vehicle has a modified half-size drivers cab on a Mercedes 2-axle chassis allowing access to low-silled aircraft. Loader platform is raised to 3.1m, suitable for aircraft types B737, A320, Tu-154.
