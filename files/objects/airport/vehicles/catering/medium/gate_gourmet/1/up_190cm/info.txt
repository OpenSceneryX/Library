Description: A mid-sized airport catering truck with platform raised to 1.9m in Gate Gourmet markings.
The vehicle has a modified half-size drivers cab on a Mercedes 2-axle chassis allowing access to low-silled aircraft. Loader platform is raised to 1.9m, suitable for aircraft types CRJ, Fokker 100, BAe146, EMB-145.
