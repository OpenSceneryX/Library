Description: A mid-sized airport catering truck with platform raised to 2.3m in Servisair markings.
The vehicle has a modified half-size drivers cab on a Mercedes 2-axle chassis allowing access to low-silled aircraft. Loader platform is raised to 2.3m, suitable for aircraft types MD-80/90, B717, EMB-170/190.
