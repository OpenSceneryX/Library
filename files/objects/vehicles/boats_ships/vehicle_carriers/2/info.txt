Description: Galaxy Leader is one of the biggest vehicle carriers. Japan-based Nippon Yusen Kaisha or NYK Line is one of the largest shipping companies in the world. It is a core Mitsubishi company. This version has open loading doors for use in port.

Speed: 20 kts, tonnage: 42,000 gross register tons (GRT).
