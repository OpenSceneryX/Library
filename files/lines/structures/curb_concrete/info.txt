Export Deprecated v4.0.0: lines/airport/structures/curb_concrete

Description: A concrete curb. Use for edges around or divisions within airport aprons.

This line is drawn at the 'Runways +5' level so should appear above all runway/taxiway surfaces but below any 'painted' markings.
