Title: Pedestrian Only Route

Export Deprecated v4.0.0: lines/airport/traffic/pedestrian_route

Description: A 'Pedestrians Only' ground marking repeating every 8m along the line path. Typically used to indicate a reserved path for passengers to take to/from aircraft on an apron.

This line is drawn at the 'Markings -1' level.
