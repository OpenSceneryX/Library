Description: A forest of dark grass, between 1m and 1.4m high. Ideal to use in combination with daisies and dandelions (/forests/plants/asteraceae). This variant is similar to /forests/grass/1 but is more warm yellowish in color tone, designed for matching the colour of some orthophotos.
